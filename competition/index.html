Time-to-Collision Estimation

This TTC benchmark includes 10 sequences, covering three main types of scenes: CCRs, CCRm, and Slider. You can directly download these data to evaluate your algorithm.

  • CCRs1-slow: hdf5 (550.1 M)   |  bbox (1 K)
  • CCRs1-medium: hdf5 (540 M)   |  bbox (1 K)
  • CCRs1-high: hdf5 (554.7 M)   |  bbox (0.9 K)
  • CCRs2-low: hdf5 (667.9 M)   |  bbox (1 K)
  • CCRs2-medium: hdf5 (861.2 M)   |  bbox (2 K)
  • CCRs2-high: hdf5 (964.9 M)   |  bbox (2 K)
  • CCRm-low: hdf5 (717.7 M)   |  bbox (1 K)
  • CCRm-medium: hdf5 (708.6 M)   |  bbox (1 K)
  • Slider-750: rosbag (516.6 M)   |  bbox (2 K)   |  calibration (61 K)
  • Slider-1000: rosbag (408.2 M)   |  bbox (0.6 K)   |  calibration (61 K)

To submit the results of your method to the EvTTC leaderboard you must first apply for submission. Every application will be manually reviewed. Furthermore, the results you submit must comply with this specific data format.


Information about datasets used by the methods.
  • 8-mm Lens Camera Pair: Method uses data collected with the 8-mm lens camera pair
  • 16-mm Lens Camera Pair: Method uses data collected with the 16-mm lens camera pair
  • Event Camera: Method uses events from Prophesee EVKv4
  • RGB Camera: Method uses images from FLIR Blackfly S
